# Business Case Documentation

This directory contains the organizational and strategic rationale for the Webmark platform.

The purpose of these documents is to frame Webmark not only as a technical solution, but as an engineering intelligence platform with operational and organizational value.

---

# Contents

## Business Case

Describes:

* the engineering problems addressed
* expected organizational benefits
* platform differentiation
* operational efficiencies
* modernization opportunities

---

## Personas

Defines intended platform users and stakeholders.

Potential personas:

* software architects
* engineering leads
* platform engineers
* modernization teams
* onboarding engineers

---

## Workflow Analysis

Documents current and future engineering workflows supported by the platform.

Examples:

* SPDD workflows
* architecture review processes
* repository analysis workflows
* engineering onboarding workflows

---

# Goals

These documents help:

* preserve organizational intent
* justify engineering investment
* align architectural decisions
* frame platform evolution
* demonstrate systems-thinking capability

---

# Engineering Perspective

The business-case documentation intentionally emphasizes:

* engineering productivity
* architecture visibility
* AI governance
* traceability
* modernization readiness

rather than purely commercial concerns.
